Customer Journey
Three steps, zero friction
📋
1. Contract Signed
Staff enters details. Customer gets a WhatsApp link with their Apple/Google Wallet pass. No app, no account.
🔧
2. Service & Booking
Customer taps "Book a Service" on their pass → request goes straight to the admin booking panel. Tech gets signed job records.
📱
3. Auto-update
After the visit, next service date advances one year. Customer’s wallet updates silently. Full audit trail, no paperwork.
