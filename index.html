How this demo works

Use "Onboard a Customer" to issue a real wallet pass — it lands in Apple Wallet in seconds. Then use the Technician Scanner to simulate a site visit. Hit "Complete Service" and watch the next service date update live on the phone. No app. No SMS. No login.

Customer Journey
Three steps, zero friction
📋
1. Contract Signed
Customer signs annual maintenance contract. Staff enters details — name, address, plan tier, account manager.
📲
2. Pass Delivered
Customer receives SMS/WhatsApp link. Taps once to add to Apple Wallet. No app, no password, no account.
🔧
3. Service & Update
Technician scans QR on-site. After service, taps "Complete" — next service date advances one year. Customer's phone updates silently.
